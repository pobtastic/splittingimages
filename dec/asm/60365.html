60365: Input Handler: Sinclair
Input
HL Points to Data_Control
IX Points to this routine
Output
A The control byte
All the handlers match the outputs of the Kempston Joystick:
Byte Bits Action
1 00000001 Right
2 00000010 Left
4 00000100 Down
8 00001000 Up
16 00010000 Fire
